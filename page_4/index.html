Why this page exists

State

Gate

Explain what changes status

A phase moves only when the evidence changes, not because the language becomes more confident.

  • Proof closes a stage.
  • Blockers keep it open.
  • Corrections update the record.

Boundaries

What this roadmap does not claim

Early proof is valuable because it is narrow. The page should make progress understandable without making it larger than it is.

  • Phase 1 does not mean public launch readiness.
  • Phase 1 does not mean final reward economics are complete.
  • Phase 1 does not mean final trust scoring is ready for user outcomes.
  • Phase 1 does not mean all support paths are automated.
  • Phase 2-5 work remains pending until the proof gates are satisfied.
  • Contributor onboarding is not a shortcut to formal authority.
  • Internal review mechanics and sensitive operating details stay off this public roadmap surface.
  • Roadmap language follows proof; it does not create proof by itself.
