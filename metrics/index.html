On-Chain Metrics

Verifiable data for evaluating token health and holder outcomes.

VISTA and BONZI are yield-generating tokens on Ethervista. Every trade on the DEX generates fees that go directly to stakers and LPs - paid in ETH, not inflationary tokens. This page shows whether that's actually happening: how much has been paid, to whom, and how ownership is distributed. All data is on-chain and verifiable.

Metrics follow Murad's memecoin criteria (token age, distribution, holder conviction) and decentralization factors relevant to regulatory classification. Data from Dune Analytics. Read the whitepaper to understand the mechanics.

What This Shows

This wallet staked 15 VISTA over a year ago and has claimed 0.086 ETH in yield - without selling a single token. This is the opposite of Ponzi behavior: the holder earned real value (ETH) from protocol fees, not from later participants. The yield came from trading activity, not from new deposits. This pattern - earning without exiting - is only possible when the underlying mechanism generates actual revenue.
