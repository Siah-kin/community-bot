3. The Ethervista Euler Model

Ethervista uses a unique fee structure called the "Euler Model" where swap fees are fixed in USD value (not percentage-based). This means:

  • The fee is the same whether you buy $50 or $5,000 worth of tokens
  • This discourages bot spam and incentivizes meaningful transactions
  • Fees are distributed to liquidity providers and the protocol treasury
  • The exact fee varies by token pool (e.g., $BONZI has ~$20 per swap)

For more information, see: docs.ethervista.app
