Quick Start

Getting started with Bonzi takes just a few steps:

  1. Open Telegram and search for @BonziVistaBot
  2. Send /start to open the dashboard
  3. Ask any question about DeFi, staking, or Ethervista
  4. Link your wallet via Settings to track rewards
Pro Tip

Bonzi remembers your conversations and adapts to your level. The more you interact, the more personalized responses become.

Commands

Here are all available commands:

Command Description
/start Open the main dashboard
/help Show available features
/settings Configure your preferences
/balance Check your token balance
/leaderboard View community rankings
/ticket Submit a support ticket

Ask Questions

Bonzi can answer questions about:

  • Tokenomics - Supply, distribution, staking mechanics
  • APR/Yields - Live rates, historical performance
  • Euler Model - Fee distribution math explained
  • Ethervista - Platform features, Etherfun launches
  • General DeFi - Liquidity, impermanent loss, etc.

Response Depth

Bonzi adapts response length based on your questions:

  • First ask - Concise 2-3 sentence answer
  • Follow-up - Explains the mechanism
  • Deep dive - Full breakdown with numbers

Leaderboard

Earn points by asking quality questions. The leaderboard tracks:

  • Question quality score (not quantity)
  • Weekly and all-time rankings
  • Top contributors get recognition
Anti-Gaming

Bonzi detects spam and low-effort questions. Quality over quantity - genuine learning earns 10x more points than farming.

Thanks System

The Thanks system lets community members reward helpful answers:

  • Reply to a helpful message with /thanks
  • Thanks are recorded on-chain
  • Top thanked users earn recognition

Settings

Customize your Bonzi experience via /settings:

  • Wallet Link - Connect your wallet for rewards
  • Personality - Choose Bonzi or Professional mode
  • Notifications - Configure alerts
  • Language - Supports 14 languages

Staking Guide

Stake $BONZI to earn ETH rewards from swap fees:

How to Stake

  1. Go to ethervista.app
  2. Connect your wallet
  3. Navigate to Staking ‚Üí $BONZI
  4. Enter amount and confirm transaction
  5. Claim rewards via the Claim button
Important

You must claim rewards before unstaking. Unclaimed rewards are forfeited if you unstake first.

APR Expectations

APR varies with trading volume. Historical range: 30-80% annually.

Troubleshooting

Bot not responding?

  • Check if the bot is online (green dot in profile)
  • In groups, make sure to tag @BonziVistaBot
  • Try /start to reset the session

Wrong balance showing?

  • Use /balance to refresh
  • Make sure correct wallet is linked in Settings
  • Wait a few minutes for blockchain sync

Still stuck?

Use /ticket to submit a support request. Include screenshots if possible.
