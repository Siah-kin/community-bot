The Problem

Quest platforms are 50-80% bots. Your real fans get diluted by farmers who disappear after claiming rewards. The engagement looks great on paper. The community is hollow.

And trading? It's zero-sum by design. For every winner, someone loses. Your community shouldn't cannibalize itself just to participate in your success.

Someone always loses. That's the design, not the bug.
