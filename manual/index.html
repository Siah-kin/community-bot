What is Bonzi?

Bonzi is an AI-powered community assistant that lives in your Telegram group. It answers questions, tracks contributions, and rewards helpful members - all without requiring moderator intervention.

Unlike generic chatbots, Bonzi learns your project's specific knowledge base and speaks in a voice that matches your community culture. It's not just a FAQ bot. It has judgment - trained on real crypto conversations spanning 3 market cycles.

Open Source: Bonzi is MIT-licensed. Self-host for free, forever. No tracking, no ads, no data harvesting. View source ‚Üí

Quick Start

Getting Bonzi running in your group takes about 2 minutes. No coding required.

  1. Add the bot: Search for @Bonzi_Community_bot in Telegram
  2. Make it admin: Bonzi needs admin permissions to read messages
  3. Send /start: Opens the dashboard in DM
  4. Done: Bonzi is now active and learning your community
Self-hosting? Clone from GitHub, set your TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN, and deploy. Full setup guide in the repo README.

Commands

All commands work in group chats and DMs. Some commands have button equivalents in the dashboard.

Command Description
/start Open the main dashboard
/ask [question] Ask Bonzi a question directly
/board View the contribution leaderboard
/thanks @user Thank someone for helping (+1 point to them)
/claim Claim ETH rewards (requires 100+ points)
/link_wallet [addr] Link your wallet for claims
/bug Report a bug (earn DAO points)
/settings Configure your preferences

Ask Questions

You can ask Bonzi questions in three ways. Pick whichever feels natural.

  • Mention: Tag @Bonzi_Community_bot followed by your question
  • Reply: Reply to any Bonzi message with a follow-up
  • Command: Use /ask followed by your question

Bonzi searches its knowledge base and generates a contextual answer. If it doesn't know something, it says so rather than hallucinate. Every answer earns you +1 point.

Learn more: Ask & Earn - Why we pay you to ask questions ‚Üí

Leaderboard

The leaderboard tracks community contributions. It's not just vanity - top contributors earn ETH claims and governance rights.

Points are earned by:

  • Asking quality questions (+1 per question)
  • Receiving thanks from other members (+1 per thanks)
  • Getting helpful reactions on your messages
  • Submitting valid bug reports (+10 DAO points)
ETH Claims: Once you hit 100 points, you can claim ETH rewards. The amount varies based on the community pool. Use /claim to check eligibility.

Thanks System

The thanks system lets you recognize helpful community members. When you thank someone, they receive a point that contributes to their leaderboard position.

/thanks @helpful_user Great explanation of staking!

Thanks is limited to prevent gaming. You can thank the same person once per day. The message after @user is optional but appreciated.

Settings

Use /settings or the dashboard to configure your experience.

  • Language: 6 languages supported (EN, PT, ZH, TR, RU, FR)
  • Notifications: Control DM frequency
  • Response style: Concise vs detailed answers
  • Wallet: Link/unlink your wallet for claims

Staking Guide

Stakers get premium features and elevated privileges. Staking also increases your claim multiplier.

  1. Visit ethervista.com
  2. Connect your wallet
  3. Choose Hardstake (locked) or LP provision (liquid)
  4. Link your wallet in Bonzi: /link_wallet 0x...
Staker Benefits:
  • Higher claim multiplier (up to 2x)
  • Priority raid verification
  • Access to premium features
  • DAO voting rights (coming soon)

Admin Tools

Group admins have access to additional controls via the /admin command or dashboard.

  • Knowledge sources: Add docs, FAQs, or custom content
  • Response parameters: Tune creativity, length, tone
  • Analytics: View question patterns, engagement stats
  • Moderation: Configure auto-responses, filter settings
  • Raids: Create and manage X raids for your community

Troubleshooting

Bonzi isn't responding

Check that Bonzi has admin permissions in your group. Without admin access, it can't read messages. Also check that the bot isn't muted or restricted.

Wrong information in answers

Claim not working

Check: (1) You have 100+ points, (2) Your wallet is linked, (3) You haven't claimed in the last 7 days, (4) The community pool has funds.
