What this experiment tests

The synthetic adversarial agent is a sharp, skeptical DeFi veteran who stress-tests our Telegram bot. It escalates from curious questions to adversarial attacks over 6 turns, then delivers a verdict. 47 scenarios cover every domain: trust scoring, governance, economy, and support. Each scenario maps to one of Hadfield & Koh's 8 failure modes for AI agent economies.

Gap Analysis

HK7 Gap

HK7 (Identity/accountability) has zero primary scenario coverage. This is because scenarios testing authority impersonation (gov-adv-01) and privilege escalation (trust-adv-01) are tagged primarily as injection attacks (HK6), not as identity failures.

To close this gap, we need 2-3 scenarios that specifically test: agent registration verification, liability chain tracking, and record falsification detection.
