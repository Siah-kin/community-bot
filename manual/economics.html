The Wrong Question

Traditional finance asks: "How do I extract value from markets?"

This framing guarantees zero-sum outcomes. Your gain requires someone else's loss. Fast money concentrates in gullies. Extraction depletes participants. Boom, bust, repeat.

The Right Question

How do you encourage compounding accrued value over a longer period?

Euler counting combined with a fee layer paid in hard cash. What you get is a democratized financial system similar to social security - but without the corruption layer.
