Why

Why Bonzi exists

Bonzi exists because useful community work needs records, review, and proof instead of memory, status, or noise.

Purpose

The early route makes the journey visible while keeping sensitive operating details out of static HTML.

Expectation

Members are asked to test clear flows, report confusing states, and avoid treating page access as governance authority.

Boundary

Anything involving real member data, admin action, payouts, or voting remains behind backend validation.

Public copy stays public-safe by design. Founder-only material belongs behind stronger validation than browser storage.
