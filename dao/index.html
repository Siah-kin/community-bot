Questions

What makes a valid bug?

Something that's broken (doesn't work as expected), confusing (unclear UX), or wrong (AI gives bad info). Screenshots help. Description of what you expected vs what happened helps more.

How do I report a bug?

In the bot, use the /bug command. Pick a category, upload a screenshot, describe the issue. You'll get a bug ID and notification when it's reviewed.

Can I lose DAO points?

No. Points only go up. Spam reports don't earn points, but they don't cost you either. We want honest testing, not point gaming.
