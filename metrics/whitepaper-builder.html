Part 0

Why This Works

Two Nobel Prizes and Vitalik agree: the problem is not decentralization itself. The problem is what you decentralize.

Traditional DAOs decentralized treasury access. The result was predictable: insiders voted themselves rich while communities watched from the sidelines. We took a different approach entirely.

Framework Authority Core Insight
Commons Governance Ostrom (Nobel 2009) Communities self-govern shared resources without central authority
Mechanism Design Hurwicz/Maskin/Myerson (Nobel 2007) Incentive structures make cooperation the rational choice
DAO Renaissance Vitalik Buterin (2025) Remove treasury, add Oracle. Governance without extraction.

What these frameworks share is a recognition that sustainable systems align individual incentives with collective benefit. When helping others becomes the most rational path to personal gain, cooperation emerges naturally.

The synthesis: We decentralized treasury removal. What remains is pure coordination. No runway to drain. No multisig to compromise. Just protocol-enforced distribution from trading fees to contributors.

Part 3

The Economics

The economic model creates a self-reinforcing loop where marketing spend becomes ETH capture. Every promotional dollar drives trades, and trades generate fees that flow back to contributors.

This inverts the traditional model where marketing is pure cost. Here, awareness generates volume, volume generates fees, and fees reward the community members who created that awareness.

Brand launches token (~$2) ‚Üì Trading fees generated (1% per trade) ‚Üì Fees ‚Üí ThanksPool ‚Üí Community claims ‚Üì Reputation earned ‚Üí Better yield multipliers ‚Üì More engagement ‚Üí More volume ‚Üí More fees ‚Üì Marketing = ETH capture (LP is only buy point)

Reputation Unlocks

Reputation is not a vanity metric. It unlocks concrete benefits: yield multipliers, governance rights, and launch priority for new features. The tiers reward sustained contribution, not one-time purchases.

Verified

Base features. Wallet linked, identity verified.

Committed

1.5x yield multiplier. Staking threshold met.

Steward

Governance + launch priority. Top contributors.

Yield multipliers are calculated from on-chain staking data combined with off-chain contribution scores. The Oracle tracks contributions (bug reports, content, support) and the smart contract applies multipliers at claim time.

Part 4

Implementation Guide

This checklist walks you through the first 30 days of launching a tokenized community. Each phase builds on the previous, so complete them in order.

Day 1 is about establishing presence. Week 1 is about building infrastructure. Month 1 is about activating the community flywheel.

Day 1: Launch

  • Launch token on Etherfun (takes 5 minutes, ~$2 gas)
  • Share contract address with your community
  • Announce on socials with clear value proposition

Week 1: Infrastructure

  • Prepare knowledge seed documents (FAQs, brand voice, values)
  • Deploy Oracle with RAG + SIAH integration
  • Configure reputation tiers for your specific community

Month 1: Activation

  • Embed wallet widget on brand website (optional)
  • Launch first community raid or campaign
  • Track metrics, iterate on engagement patterns
