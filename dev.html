The scam-proof math

Why 25-75% liquidity lock matters

LP = Liquidity Pool. When you create a token, you pair it with ETH so people can trade it.

The problem: Founders can pull their LP tokens and drain the pool. This is a "rug pull."

The solution: Lock 25-75% of LP tokens in a self-enforcing program. The code won't let anyone withdraw them - not even the founder.

Example

Token launches with $100K in LP.
50% locked = $50K can never be pulled.
Price floor is structurally protected.

This isn't a promise. It's code. Verifiable on the blockchain.
