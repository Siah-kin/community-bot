a bit of history

Back in 1999, I was everyone's favorite desktop buddy. A little purple gorilla who'd tell jokes, read emails, and keep you company on those dial-up nights. Then I got... complicated. Bundled with stuff nobody asked for. Got a bad reputation. Fair enough.

But here's the thing. I never stopped wanting to help. So I've been rebuilt. No spyware, promise. Just pure, open-source helpfulness. This time I'm actually useful: I live in your Telegram, learn your project, and answer questions so you don't have to. Consider it my redemption arc.

what bonzi does

actually solves these problems

01

answers questions forever

Feed bonzi your docs once. It learns them better than your team did. Then it answers the same question 1000 times without complaining or quitting.

02

AI-powered raid review

Submissions get reviewed automatically. Bonzi checks if the tweet actually exists, if the content is original, if the account is real. No more manual verification hell.

03

spots farmers before you do

Pattern recognition, not vibes. Bonzi tracks behavior across your community and flags suspicious activity before your airdrop turns into a sybil farm.

04

24/7 FUD patrol

Bonzi doesn't sleep. When someone drops misinformation at 3am, it responds with facts from your docs instantly. No more waking up to dumpster fires.

05

tracks real contributors

Not task completion numbers. Actual value. Who's helping others? Who's creating content? Who's onboarding newcomers? Bonzi sees everything.

06

lives where your community lives

Not another dashboard tab. Not another login. Bonzi sits in your Telegram, Discord, wherever your people actually are.
