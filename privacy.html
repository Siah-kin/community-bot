Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Bonzi is a Telegram AI bot that helps communities manage engagement, verify contributions, and reward real participants. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

The short version

We collect what we need to make the bot work. We don't sell your data. We don't track you across the web. Wallet address only - no emails, no names, no payment details stored.

What We Collect

Data you provide directly

  • Messages and questions sent to the bot
  • Ratings and feedback on responses
  • Telegram user ID and chat ID
  • Commands you execute
  • Wallet addresses (for claims)

Data collected automatically

  • Usage frequency and session patterns
  • Quality scores and behavioral signals
  • Technical metadata and timestamps

What we DON'T collect

  • Personal names (unless you share them)
  • Email addresses
  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone numbers
  • Payment card details

How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to:

  • Answer your questions and process commands
  • Improve response quality over time
  • Detect sybil attacks and abuse patterns
  • Calculate contribution scores fairly
  • Process ETH claims for verified contributors

AI Processing

Messages are processed through AI providers (OpenAI GPT, Anthropic Claude) to generate responses. We configure these services to prevent your data from being used for model training where possible.

Our quality improvement loop uses anonymized feedback patterns - not your specific messages - to identify gaming behavior and improve fairness.

Storage & Security

  • Database: PostgreSQL on Render.com (US region)
  • Caching: Redis for performance
  • Backups: Cloudflare R2
  • Security: Encryption at rest and in transit

Retention periods

  • Messages: Kept indefinitely for context
  • Logs: Deleted after 30 days
  • Cached responses: 7 days

Third Parties

We share data only with:

  • AI providers (for message processing)
  • Hosting platforms (for infrastructure)

We never sell data to advertisers or data brokers.

Your Rights

You can request:

  • Access: Get a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Remove your data (processed within 30 days)
  • Correction: Fix inaccurate information

Contact us via Telegram at @ethervistatoken for any requests.

Compliance

This policy complies with:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California)
  • Telegram's Third-Party Bot Privacy Policy requirements

Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced in our Telegram community. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
