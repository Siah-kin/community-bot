BONZI
Meme + CPI Cooperation Scoring

Bonzi is a memecoin built on Ethervista (flat ETH fees, not percentage). It's self-aware about risk—that's why we built CPI cooperation scoring as the safety layer. The positioning: "I know it's risky, but that's why we built THIS."

Mechanics: Euler fee model generates flat $10-15 ETH per transaction, distributed to LP providers. Protocol auto-buys and burns BONZI with a portion of fees (deflationary). Hardstake: lock tokens 14+ days for larger share of fee pool. CPI scoring gates marketing rewards based on cooperation signals—helpful answers, quality contributions, on-chain reputation.

The moat: wallet cooperation scoring (CPI) vs. Nansen flow analysis and Arkham identity tagging. CPI tracks 7 years of behavioral history across 13 signals. Nansen shows who's trading, Arkham shows who they are, CPI shows if they're trustworthy.

Wallet Cooperation vs. Transaction Flows

Nansen tracks on-chain flows (wash trading unclear). Arkham tags identities (reputation opaque). CPI tracks behavioral history—helpful answers, quality contributions, staking duration, tip patterns. 10 dimensions, anti-gameable by design. 7 years of history per wallet.

IMOBX
RWA Tokenization Primitive

IMOBX is an RWA primitive for impact construction. Tokenized housing projects + rent-to-own financing, designed as reusable infrastructure for jurisdiction-agnostic compliance. Not just "tokenized real estate"—a framework other RWA DAOs can adopt.

Mechanics: Dual yield model—LP trading fees (like Bonzi) + rental income from financed properties. DAO governance controls revenue allocation, project selection, and SINAPI tracking (Brazil's government construction cost index). Rent-to-own financing: tenants rent with option to buy, building equity over time. Regulatory edge: modular compliance layer adapts to local housing laws (Brazil SA structure, US DAO unincorporated, EU MiCA utility token).

The moat: SINAPI integration + Social Impact focus (SDG 11 housing goal). While other RWA protocols chase tokenized treasuries and real estate speculation, IMOBX targets grant-eligible social housing. This creates a regulatory moat—governments prefer impact projects over pure profit plays. Example: IDB $250M Social Impact Fund prioritizes affordable housing, not luxury condos.

SINAPI + Social Impact Grant Leverage

SINAPI is Brazil's government construction cost index—publicly auditable, updated monthly, used for official procurement. IMOBX uses it to prove construction costs ($27,777 per unit in Jardim Guadalupe). Combined with Rent-to-Own model serving vulnerable populations, this unlocks SDG 11 grant eligibility (IDB, BNDES, climate funds). Traditional RWA platforms can't access these—they're profit-only.

UNIDOS
NGO + Reputation Staking

Unidos is an NGO leveraging on-chain reputation staking for newcomer vetting. Award-based authentication (UNHCR Gene Dewey Award, Lush Prize, government IDs) creates a trust transfer chain—verified members stake their reputation to vet newcomers, earning commissions on successful vets.

Mechanics: Award verification—6 official documents (UNHCR card, passport, NGO certificates) = 270 baseline reputation points. Reputation staking: stake points to vouch for others, earn marketplace commissions on successful vets (3-5% of task fees). Vetting funnel: newcomers submit proof → Unidos 270pt holders verify → AI oracle confirms behavioral consistency. Slashing: fraudulent vouching loses staked points (anti-abuse mechanism).

The moat: award-based authentication vs. self-attestation (WorldCoin, Proof of Humanity). WorldCoin relies on iris scans (privacy risk, centralized database). Proof of Humanity uses video + vouching (gameable via deepfakes, low-cost Fiverr attacks). Unidos requires official government/UNHCR documents—expensive to forge ($5K+ on black market), easy to verify via cryptographic signatures. The moat is real-world paper trail + stake-to-vouch economics.

Award-Based Auth vs. Biometric/Video Vouching

WorldCoin (iris scans): Privacy risk, centralized Worldcoin Foundation controls database, orb dependency. Proof of Humanity (video + vouching): Gameable via deepfakes, Fiverr vouching farms, no skin-in-game for fraudulent vouchers. Unidos (official docs + reputation staking): UNHCR Gene Dewey Award + government IDs = cryptographically verifiable, $5K+ to forge, staking creates accountability (lose 50pts if newcomer = fraud). Pan-African expansion: 6-award holders become regional vetting committees.
