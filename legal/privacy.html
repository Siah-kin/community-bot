Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

We collect what we need to run the bot. We do not sell data and we do not track users across the web.

What We Collect

Data you provide directly

  • Messages and questions sent to the bot
  • Ratings and feedback on responses
  • Telegram user ID and chat ID
  • Commands you execute
  • Wallet addresses (for claims)

Data collected automatically

  • Usage frequency and session patterns
  • Quality scores and behavioral signals
  • Technical metadata and timestamps

What we do NOT collect

  • Personal names (unless you share them)
  • Email addresses
  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone numbers
  • Payment card details

How We Use Your Data

  • Answer questions and process commands
  • Improve response quality over time
  • Detect sybil attacks and abuse patterns
  • Calculate contribution scores
  • Process ETH claims for verified users

AI Processing

Messages are processed by AI providers to generate replies. Where possible, we configure providers to avoid model training on your data.

Quality improvement uses anonymized patterns, not direct replay of your private chat text.

Storage and Security

  • Database: PostgreSQL on Render (US)
  • Caching: Redis
  • Backups: Cloudflare R2
  • Security: Encryption at rest and in transit

Retention

  • Messages: retained for context
  • Logs: 30 days
  • Cached responses: 7 days

Your Rights

  • Access: request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: request deletion (processed within 30 days)
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate data

Contact: @Bonzivista_bot

Compliance and Changes

This policy is maintained for GDPR, CCPA, and Telegram third-party bot privacy requirements.

If we change this policy, we publish the update date here and announce material changes in the Telegram community.
