Frequently Asked Questions

What is BONZI Staking?
Lock your BONZI tokens to secure the network and earn ETH rewards directly from protocol activity.
Ethervista and this page
This screen is Bonzivista staking (tabs above). Ethervista is separate software and branding. Their overview is here if you want it: ethervista.app (how it works).
How are rewards paid?
ETH rewards accrue in real time. Claim them from the Claim tab above when you are ready (wallet transaction required).
Is there a lock period?
Yes, there is a 14-day withdrawal lock for staked BONZI to ensure pool stability. Claiming ETH does not reset that timer.
How is APR calculated?
Numbers come from the public staking metrics JSON and often include more than one type of percent. Some of those mixes are misleading when liquidity is thin, so sometimes this page shows how much ETH was paid over time (ETH per year, a backward pace), not a big percent headline. When metrics look stable we may show one pool-wide percent instead. Connecting your wallet does not change those pool tiles; they stay file-based until refreshed.
